Who I Am

I’m a data professional with interests spanning data analytics, business intelligence, data science, and data engineering. I enjoy turning raw data into clear insights, scalable workflows, and decision-ready solutions through a combination of technical implementation and business problem-solving.

My work focuses on SQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau, and Microsoft Fabric, with experience in data cleaning, dimensional modeling, dashboard development, machine learning, and end-to-end analytics workflows. I’m especially interested in projects that connect data to real business questions across domains such as operations, retail, finance, and supply chain.

I’m a University of California, San Diego alumnus and I’m currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology, with a focus on Computational Science.
